Maglev:


The maglev system is responsible for developing and implementing the magnetic levitation subsystems as well as aiding in lateral control through the use of magnets.



Suspension:


The suspension subsystem is responsible for designing and implementing the chasis design, wheel subsystem and the braking mechanisms for the pod.



Fuselage:


The fuselage subsystem is responsible for designing the casing and shape of the outermost shell. The take into consideration material costs, drag and other physical factors.



Electrical:


The electrical subsystem is responsible for connecting all subsystems electronically as well as reading the data from the pods sensors. The pods sensors deliver data that drive the self correcting safety features on board the pod.
